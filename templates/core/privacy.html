Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 23, 2025

J.E.M – Just Eat More ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our website, mobile app, or services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide when ordering, contacting us, or using our services:

  • Order information: Name, phone number, WhatsApp number, and pickup or delivery location
  • Push notifications (optional): If you enable notifications, we store the subscription data (endpoint and keys) needed to send them
  • Suggestions and feedback: Name, phone (optional), and message when you submit a suggestion

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Process and fulfil your orders
  • Contact you about your orders (e.g. WhatsApp, phone)
  • Send order status updates and payment reminders (if you have opted in)
  • Respond to your suggestions and feedback
  • Improve our services and user experience

3. Children's Privacy

Our snack bundles are intended for use by families. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under 13. Orders and account activity are conducted by parents or guardians. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information only as needed to operate our business, for example with delivery partners (e.g. Knutsford Express) or payment-related services. We may also share information if required by law.

5. Data Security

We use appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your data. Our site is served over HTTPS. We retain your information only as long as needed for orders, support, and legal obligations.

6. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information. You can withdraw push notification consent at any time through your device or browser. To exercise these rights, contact us using the details below.

7. Third-Party Services

Our app and website may link to or use third-party services such as WhatsApp for ordering and communication. Their use of your data is governed by their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review those policies.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the updated policy on this page and update the "Last updated" date. Continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your personal information, contact us:
